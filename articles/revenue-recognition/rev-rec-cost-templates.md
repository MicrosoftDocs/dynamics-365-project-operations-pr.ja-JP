9. <span data-ttu-id="4b080-179">完了割合の計算に使用する完了経費の計算方法を選択します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4b080-179">Select the cost to complete method to be used when calculating the percentage of completion.</span></span>
